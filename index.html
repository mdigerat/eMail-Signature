How-To

Download version suitable for print
  1. Select your campus - Philadelphia (PA) or Georgia (GA) from the tabs.
  2. Fill out each of the form's fields and tab to the next field noticing as you tab that your information replaces the default information in the signature
  3. Then select and copy the formatted signature that is generated with your information at the bottom of the form
    1. Press and hold left mouse button starting from right-hand side of the LinkedIn icon and drag up to the left-hand side of your name
    2. Right-click and select copy from the menu
  4. It will then be placed onto your clipboard and can then be pasted into your Google email client as follows
    1. Select the in the upper right-hand corner of Google Mail
    2. From the dropdown select
    3. From the Settings page scroll down untill you see the Signature section
    4. Right-click inside the signature window and from the pop-up select
    5. Your new signature should look similar to the signature below - (but with your information)
    6. Scroll to the bottom until you see the button and then click to save your new signature
  5. The final step is to make sure our new signature is indeed working.
    1. Select the button to bring up a New email message
    2. Verify the draft email has been updated to your new signature - CONGRATULATIONS!
